How We Help

More Than “Just a Contractor”

Most contractors only build what the adjuster writes. We help you understand what should be there in the first place.

AI‑Assisted Estimate Review

We feed your estimate into AI‑powered tools and compare it against local code, manufacturer specs, and real‑world pricing to flag missing or under‑paid items.

Built-Right Supplements

Supplements are written with photos, measurements, and code citations (WRB, flashing, ventilation, frieze wrap, etc.) that show adjusters exactly what the job actually requires.

Adjuster‑Ready Documentation

Every phase – inspection, build, and final – is documented so you have proof of work done and materials used for your carrier, lender, or a future buyer.

Homeowner‑Friendly Explanations

We explain the estimate in plain language: what’s covered, what’s missing, and what options you have so you can make confident decisions.
