Insurance claim help for storm damage—clear, organized, homeowner-first

Estimates are hard to read. Line items do not always match real labor and materials. Jespersen Erections offers insurance claim help so you understand what is in your scope, what may be missing, and what documentation supports a fair outcome. We do not replace your insurance company—we help you present accurate information.

How it works

AI-assisted review & adjuster-ready documentation

What “AI-assisted” means

We use tools to help compare your carrier’s scope to typical repair needs and code-appropriate details. It supports our judgment—it does not guarantee payment and it does not replace your adjuster.

Adjuster-ready documentation

Photos, measurements, and notes that tell one story: what was damaged, where, and what it takes to fix it built-right. That cuts down on back-and-forth.

What you gain

Clearer reading of your estimate, fewer surprises after work starts, and confidence that your scope reflects real exterior restoration needs—not bare-minimum patches.

What we do not do

We do not promise claim outcomes or encourage fraud. We do not act as a public adjuster unless separately licensed to do so in your jurisdiction. We help you present complete, accurate information.
