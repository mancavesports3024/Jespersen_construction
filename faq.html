FAQ

Common questions

Insurance, services, service area, and how we work. For a step-by-step overview, see our process.

Do you help with insurance claims after hail or wind in Joplin?

Yes. We help with documentation, supplements, and adjuster-ready photos and measurements for roofing and exteriors. We do not replace your insurance company’s decisions.

What services do you offer?

Roof repair and roof replacement, storm restoration, siding and gutters, and AI-assisted insurance estimate review. Licensed and insured, based in Joplin, MO.

What areas do you serve outside Joplin, MO?

We serve the four-state area around Joplin including Southwest Missouri, Northwest Arkansas, Northeast Oklahoma, and Southeast Kansas. Call to confirm coverage for your address.

What does built-right construction mean?

We install roofing, siding, and drainage components to code and manufacturer standards—underlayment, flashing, ventilation, and related details included—not shortcuts hidden under shingles.

How do I request a storm damage inspection?

Call 417-396-9941 or use our contact form. We schedule as soon as we can, especially after widespread storms.
