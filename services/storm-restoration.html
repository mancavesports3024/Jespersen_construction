How We Restore Your Home

Clear stages, clear communication, and documentation you can rely on.

1. Storm Inspection & Documentation

We walk the roof, exterior, and property, capturing photos, measurements, and notes on every damaged or code‑deficient item – not just what’s on the first estimate.

2. AI‑Assisted Estimate Review

Your carrier’s scope is compared against local code, manufacturer specs, and real‑world costs with help from AI tools so we can spot what’s missing and prepare supplements.

3. Supplements & Adjuster‑Ready Docs

We write supplements with clear photos, measurements, and code citations so adjusters can see exactly what’s needed and why – reducing back‑and‑forth and surprises.

4. Built-Right Construction with Catch-All System

Roofing, siding, gutters, and carpentry are installed to code and manufacturer specs while our catch‑all system protects lawns, plants, rock beds, and flatwork from debris.

5. Final Walkthrough & Documentation

You get a final walkthrough, nail sweep, and photo set you can keep for your records, your adjuster, or a future buyer – proof of exactly what was done.
