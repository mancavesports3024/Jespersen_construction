Six steps

What happens when you hire Jespersen Erections

1. Contact

You call or use our contact form. We ask what you see, recent weather, and any paperwork from your carrier. We schedule a storm damage inspection when it makes sense.

2. On-site inspection

We walk the roof and exterior, note damage patterns, and photograph problem areas. We explain findings in plain language—no jargon required.

3. Scope & options

We outline what needs repair or roof replacement and how it fits code and manufacturer rules. If you are using insurance, we map damage to line items carriers recognize.

4. Documentation

When applicable, we prepare adjuster-ready photos, measurements, and notes for insurance claim help—so everyone works from the same facts.

5. Construction

We protect your property during tear-off (including catch-all debris control where needed), then complete work to spec. You get updates as we go.

6. Walkthrough

We review the finished work with you, answer questions, and close out only when you understand what was done.
