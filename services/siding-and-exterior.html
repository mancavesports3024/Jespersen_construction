Common Problems We Fix

Beyond What the Adjuster Can See

A quick drive‑by inspection doesn’t always catch warped panels, pulled nails, or water paths behind your siding and trim. We look for the code‑level issues that cause future rot and paint failure.

  • Missing or improperly lapped WRB and flashing behind siding
  • Damaged or undersized gutters that dump water next to the house
  • Trim and fascia that were never sealed or flashed correctly
  • Unprotected landscaping and hardscapes under tear‑off work
