What we handle

Hail damage repair Joplin · wind · leaks · full re-roofs

We treat the roof as a system: deck, underlayment, shingles (or other coverings), flashing, and airflow. Built-right means code and manufacturer rules—not skipped layers because they are out of sight.

Inspection

We inspect shingles, flashing, decking, ventilation, and penetrations. We document damage with photos and notes for your records and for storm damage inspection after hail or wind.

Built-right installation

Underlayment, ice and water shield, flashing, and ventilation installed to local code—not “whatever the last crew did.”

Insurance paperwork

If you are filing a claim, we help align the scope with real damage. Our insurance claim help includes organized line items and photos adjusters can use—without pressure tactics.

Debris control

We use catch-all style protection so tear-off does not trash your landscaping or your neighbor’s yard.

Why it matters

Shortcuts cost more later

Many storm jobs look fine from the street but hide missing underlayment, bad flashing, or weak ventilation. Those show up later as leaks and denied follow-up claims. We do not build that way.
