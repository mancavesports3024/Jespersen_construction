What’s Included

Trusted Roof Replacement & Repair

From shingles to sheathing and ventilation, we restore your roof as a complete system – not just the most visible pieces.

Detailed Roof Inspection

We inspect the whole system – shingles, flashing, decking, ventilation, penetrations, and accessories – documenting every damaged or code‑deficient item with photos and notes.

Built-Right Installation

Underlayments, ice & water shields, flashing, ventilation, and fasteners installed to manufacturer specs and local building code, not just “what the last contractor did.”

AI‑Assisted Estimate Review

Your insurance estimate is checked with AI tools and our field experience to spot missing line items, under‑paid quantities, and code upgrades that should be included.

Catch‑All Debris Protection

We use a catch‑all system that funnels shingles, nails, and torn‑off materials into contained chutes – protecting landscaping, rock beds, driveways, and neighboring property.

Why It Matters

Not All Storm Roofs Are Built the Same

Many storm jobs look good from the street but hide shortcuts under the shingles – missing underlayments, improper flashing, bad ventilation, or skipped code requirements. Those shortcuts show up later as leaks, rot, and denied claims.

  • Full tear‑off and substrate evaluation instead of “nail‑over” shortcuts
  • Valleys, walls, chimneys, and penetrations rebuilt with proper flashing
  • Ventilation and intake balanced to manufacturer specs
  • Final documentation you can hand to your adjuster or future buyer
